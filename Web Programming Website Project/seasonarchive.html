SEASON ARCHIVE

From a totally tropical Jungle Adventure to a Medieval realm of Magical Mayhem, every Fall Guys season brought a new-look Blunderdome and fresh rounds to overcome as you stumbled towards victory. Let’s take a look back at our previous Seasons!




SEASON 1

ULTIMATE KNOCKOUT

This is where it all began… Season 1 of Ultimate Knockout had Fall Guys stumbling into the world, full of colorful classic rounds including Door Dash, See Saw and Slime Climb!


SEASON 2

MEDIEVAL KNOCKOUT

Season 2 of Ultimate Knockout got Fall Guys players diving across the drawbridge to enter the feudal fortress of medieval mayhem! Multiple new Rounds joined the fray, including Wall Guys and Egg Siege, plus the introduction of the Show Selector.


SEASON 3

WINTER KNOCKOUT

Season 3 of Ultimate Knockout was a chilly one. Players wrapped up their beans and got ready to slip, stumble and slide their way to the Crown! Wintry-themed Season 3 showcased Crown Ranks, alongside icy fresh Rounds such as Ski Fall and Pegwin Pursuit.


SEASON 4

4041

Season 4 of Ultimate Knockout brought players through time, to a Blunderdome full of synthetic beats and fluorescent treats! Squads mode and Challenges made their debut, alongside Skyline Stumble, Big Shots and other retro-futuristic Rounds.


SEASON 5

JUNGLE ADVENTURE


Season 5 of Ultimate Knockout got players ready to stumble into the jungle! Fully equipped with an adventurous jungle adventure theme, Season 5 showcased the likes of Stompin’ Ground, Lost Temple and our first ever Limited Time Events!


SEASON 6

PARTY SPECTACULAR


Season 6 of Ultimate Knockout invited Fall Guys players to the Party Spectacular! We dove into a festival-fresh Blunderdome, celebrating with fun rounds like Pipe Dream, Party Promenade and many other super-colorful treats!
